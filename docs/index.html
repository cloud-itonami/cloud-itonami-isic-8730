ISIC 8730 — Open Business Blueprint

Residential care activities for the elderly and disabled

An open, governed actor for assisted-living operators: resident intake, jurisdiction assessment, care-plan finalization and incident-response proposals, backed by an immutable audit ledger. The Eldercare Governor independently verifies every finalization proposal against spec-cited jurisdiction requirements, regulatory review intervals and incident resolution status — hard holds enforce compliance, and a human facility administrator always signs off before any care plan or incident response is finalized. Deploy this as a forkable business model on your own infrastructure.
